Mashed Potatoes Recipe

Description:
Mashed potatoes are a classic comfort food made by mashing boiled potatoes and mixing them with butter, milk, and seasoning for a creamy, smooth texture. They're perfect as a side dish for meats, gravies, and vegetables.

Ingredients:
- 4 large potatoes (Yukon Gold or Russet)
- 4 tablespoons butter
- 1/2 cup milk
- Salt to taste
- Pepper to taste
- Optional: garlic, cream, chives

Steps:
1. Peel the potatoes and cut them into even chunks.
2. Boil the potatoes in a large pot with salted water until tender (about 15-20 minutes).
3. Drain the potatoes and return them to the pot.
4. Add butter and mash the potatoes until smooth.
5. Slowly stir in the milk, adjusting the consistency as desired.
6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
7. (Optional) Mix in garlic, cream, or chopped chives for extra flavor.
8. Serve hot.
