Burger Recipe

Description:
A homemade burger is a juicy beef patty grilled to perfection and served on a toasted bun with your favorite toppings. It's a quick and satisfying meal for any occasion.

Ingredients:
- 1 lb ground beef (80/20)
- Salt and pepper
- 4 hamburger buns
- 4 slices cheese (optional)
- Lettuce
- Tomato, sliced
- Onion, sliced
- Pickles
- Ketchup, mustard, mayo

Steps:
1. Preheat a grill or skillet over medium-high heat.
2. Divide ground beef into 4 equal portions and form into patties.
3. Season each side with salt and pepper.
4. Cook patties for about 3-4 minutes per side, until desired doneness.
5. Add cheese slices on top in the last minute of cooking (optional).
6. Toast buns on the grill or in a pan until lightly browned.
7. Assemble the burger: bun, patty, lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, and condiments.
8. Serve immediately with fries or a side salad.
