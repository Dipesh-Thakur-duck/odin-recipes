Burger Recipe

Description

A burger is a juicy beef patty grilled to perfection and served on a toasted bun with your favorite toppings. It's a quick and satisfying meal for any occassion.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef (80% lean)
  • Salt and pepper
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 4 slices of cheese (optional)
  • Lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles (for toppings)
  • Ketchup, mustard, mayonnaise (for condiments)

Steps:

  1. Preheat grill or stovetop skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Divide ground beef into 4 equal portions and form into patties.
  3. Season each side with salt and pepper.
  4. Cook patties on the grill or skillet for about 3-4 minutes per side for medium doneness.
  5. If using cheese, place a slice on each patty during the last minute of cooking to melt.
  6. Toast the hamburger buns on the grill or in a toaster.
  7. Assemble the burger by placing the patty on the bottom bun and adding desired toppings and condiments.
  8. Top with the other half of the bun and serve hot.
